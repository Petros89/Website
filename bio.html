I was born on 01/10/1989 in Athens - Greece and obtained my Diploma in Mechanical Engineering (5-year BSc & MSc joint degree; 300 ECTS) School of National Technical University of Athens - Greece (NTUA) on November 2015. I came at Pittsburgh, PA - USA on 08/07/2018 to pursue my graduate (MSc) program in Mechanical Engineering at Swanson School of Engineering - University of Pittburgh. My studies are mainly focused on High Performance Computing (HPC) for Engineering Simulations aiming at optimal design for large-scale computations - applications acceleration and high-fidelity predictions. During my graduate studies i have been training on parallel progamming using distributed memory parallelism (MPI) on clusters and Graphics Processing Units (GPUs). During my studies I worked as an Intern Software (R&D) Testing Engineer at ANSYS Inc in Canonsburgh from 05/13/2019 to 12/13/2019. My main goals at ANSYS were dealing with computing the performance and testing of the HPC finite element solvers of ANSYS on several Linux and Windows clusters and GPUs. I also worked on the development and product testing of an automated regression tool for the parallelization of the execution of large QA lists containing thousands of programs on Linux clusters by utilizing the Univa Grid Engine (UGE) scheduler on "lustre" shared-file system location. Last but not least, i enjoy playing soccer and music (piano,drums,mpouzouki).
